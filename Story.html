Story

Hello, friends! My new album "Distant lights" drifts beyond familiar constellations. It is an auditory map of interstellar silence, weaving slow-evolving drones with the shimmering textures of the cosmos.

Inspired by gravitational waves and the slow ballet of light/shadow, these soundscapes themselves inspire reflection and promote a sonic journey of the quiet, immense void.

Delicate field recordings are reimagined as solar wind, intertwined with soft, resonant sonic loops, best listened to during the quiet hours. thanks for listening! Josephine.
