Mission

With this album my mission was to create a sonic tapestry, evoking internal conversations and quiet thought. With these soundscapes I hope you find a space that was always there, a space for reflection and calm.

Using layered drones and continuous piano, the concept of this album is to see beyond, into the ether, into space. It is a catalyst for introspection, all the while creating other-worldly sparse textures for the immersive abstract. Thank you for listening. Josephine.
